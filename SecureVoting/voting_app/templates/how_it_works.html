How SecureVote Works

1

📝 Voter Registration

Citizens must register using their valid details. The system creates a secure profile linked directly to your authentic Email ID and Phone Number.

2

🔐 OTP Verification (2FA)

To access the voting dashboard, users must log in and verify their identity via a 6-digit dynamic OTP sent directly to their registered email.

3

🆔 Aadhaar & Live Face Scan

Before casting a vote, the user enters their 12-digit Aadhaar. The system then activates the webcam to capture a Live Face Scan. An AI algorithm generates a unique face-hash to prevent duplicate voting.

4

✅ Vote Recorded Securely

Once identity is verified against existing records, the vote is permanently encrypted and recorded in the database. A single user can never vote twice!
